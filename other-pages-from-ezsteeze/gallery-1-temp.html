About Us

Play It Again Sports has been the leading sporting goods store in Lynnwood for the last 30+ years! We feel fortunate to be a part of this vibrant community and are active and committed to promoting healthy activities for our kids. We are proud to be a local, family-owned and operated business franchise of Play It Again Sports®. What separates us from other sporting goods stores is that we carry gently used equipment as well as new gear, and we have a well-trained, knowledgable staff that is eager to help you and your family! We have one of the largest hockey inventories in Western Washington and are a big contributor to the Ski / Snowboard game with a full service shop, ski and snowboard daily and seasonal rentals, along with new and gently used skis and snowboards for sale. We pride ourselves on the best customer service and are looking for ways to give our customers a more fulfilling experience! Julie and John, Owners
